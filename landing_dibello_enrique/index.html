About

sability testing is a crucial step in the process of designing interfaces that are easy and intuitive to use. Without direct feedback from users, your design efforts and iterations are merely guesswork. This month, you will design a series of interfaces intended for specific use. With the help of usability testing, you will see your interfaces through the eyes of test participants. More specifically, the usability tests created this month will reveal where users click when navigating your interfaces, while trying to accomplish a task or goal. The tasks (interactions) you design for users should reflect the business and user goals defined early in the “Discovery” phase of product development.
