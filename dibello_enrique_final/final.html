About Us

Because we are a Schweizer Distributor, you'll have the luxury of training in the newest Schweizer 300 series helicopters. The 300 series has the finest safety record of any FAA certified, piston-powered helicopter which is substantiated by the National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) accident statistics. Our equipment is meticulously maintained to factory standards by our factory-trained personnel. Tomlinson Aviation is committed to providing you with the safest, most thorough training available. We are a Part 135 operator, so we understand how commercial operators work and can transfer this knowledge to our students. At Tomlinson, you will learn from instructors who are out slaying pipeline patrols and air charter, and as a result you will receive practical and in-depth training.

Mission

We aim to provide the student with the best possible learning environment and quality instruction at the lowest possible cost.

Courses

Private Pilote License (PPL)

This course begins the initial journey of your helicopter training and will be your formal introduction to the world of flying. This will also be the first step of your training to become a professional helicopter pilot.

Commercial Pilot License (CPL)

This course is mean to prepare you to the real world jobs. With a commercial license you will be able to get paid as a pilot working for an airline, off-shore, emergency services or private.
