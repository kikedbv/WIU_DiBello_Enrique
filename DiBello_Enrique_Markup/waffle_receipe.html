Waffle Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

Important

Follow step by step the following:

  1. Preheat waffle iron. Beat eggs in a large bowl with hand mixer until fluffy.
  2. Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, just until smooth.
  3. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray.
  4. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron.
  5. Cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot.
